The Workshop About PROCON

The International Workshop on Programmability for Cloud Networks and Applications (PROCON) is a specialized academic workshop focused on the programmability, automation, and intelligent orchestration of cloud network infrastructures and distributed applications. Held in conjunction with the International Teletraffic Congress (ITC) — the first international conference in networking science and practice, founded in 1955 — PROCON brings together researchers and practitioners working at the intersection of cloud computing, software-defined networking, network function virtualization, and intelligent edge systems.

Each edition features a Best Paper Award selected by the Technical Program Committee based on originality, technical rigor, and potential for real-world impact. The 2024 edition recognized outstanding contributions in the area of adaptive, intelligent load balancing for multi-tier edge-cloud architectures.
