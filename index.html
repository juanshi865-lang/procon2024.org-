The Workshop About PROCON

The International Workshop on Programmability for Cloud Networks and Applications (PROCON) is a specialized academic workshop dedicated to advancing the state of the art in programmable cloud network infrastructures, intelligent edge-to-cloud systems, and software-defined application delivery. PROCON serves as a focused forum where researchers from academia and industry present and discuss innovative solutions at the intersection of cloud computing, software-defined networking (SDN), network function virtualization (NFV), and intelligent edge systems.

Each edition of PROCON presents a Best Paper Award to recognize the most outstanding contribution among the accepted papers. The award is selected by the Technical Program Committee through a rigorous evaluation process based on originality, technical rigor, experimental validation, and potential for real-world impact. The 2024 edition particularly emphasized contributions to adaptive, intelligent control of multi-tier cloud-edge infrastructures.
